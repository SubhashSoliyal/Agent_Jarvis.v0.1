Subhash aur Ruchi ka Samvaad
Ek Anokhe Grah ki Khoj

Subhash: Ruchi, tune kabhi socha hai ki humare saur mandal ke baahar kitne ajeeb-o-gareeb grah honge?

Ruchi: Haan Subhash, maine suna hai ki humare brahmand mein lagbhag 100-200 arab grah hain. Lekin humne abhi tak sirf kuch hi grahon ki khoj ki hai.

Subhash: Theek hai, lekin kal humne ek aise grah ki khoj ki hai jo poore brahmand mein apne aap mein anokha hai. Us grah ka naam hai Xylophia-IV.

Ruchi: Xylophia-IV? Yeh naam mujhe bahut ajeeb lag raha hai. Is grah ke baare mein aur batao.

Subhash: Xylophia-IV ek aisa grah hai jahan par humne kabhi socha bhi nahi tha ki jeevan ho sakta hai. Yeh grah poore tarah se barf se dhaka hua hai, lekin uske neeche ek bada samudra hai jo poore tarah se garam pani se bhara hua hai.

Ruchi: Yeh toh bahut hi romanchak hai! Aapne is grah ki khoj kaise ki?

Subhash: Humne apne advanced spaceship, Aurora, ka upyog karke is grah ki khoj ki hai. Yeh spaceship humein 20 hazar prati varsh ki gati se yatra karne ki kshamta deti hai.

Ruchi: Wah, yeh toh bahut tezi se hai! Aur is grah par aapne kya-kya dekha?

Subhash: Humne is grah par kayi ajeeb cheezein dekhi hain. Jaise ki, wahan par humne ek aisi jhadi dekhi hai jo apne aap ko badalti rehti hai. Ek samay woh jhadi ek ped ki tarah dikhti hai, aur doosre samay woh jhadi ek jaanwar ki tarah dikhti hai.

Ruchi: Yeh toh bahut hi rahasyamay hai! Aapne is grah par aur kya-kya dekha?

Subhash: Humne is grah par ek aisa sheher dekha hai jo poore tarah se swachh aur sundar hai. Wahan par humne kayi ajeeb-o-gareeb imarten dekhi hain jo humne kabhi dekhi nahi thi.

Ruchi: Yeh toh bahut hi accha hai! Aapne is grah ki khoj ke baad kya mahsoos kiya?

Subhash: Humne is grah ki khoj ke baad yeh mahsoos kiya ki humare brahmand mein abhi bahut kuch hai jo humne kabhi socha bhi nahi tha. Aur humen lagta hai ki yeh grah humein bahut kuch sikha sakta hai.
